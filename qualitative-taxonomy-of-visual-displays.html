A Qualitative Taxonomy of Visual Displays

I did a review of a lot of commercial and research displays, like AR, VR displays and classical flat screen displays on phones, tablets, TVs, and more. I categorized the displays as I saw best, which resulted in an unusual set of classifications, but ones that I thought best represented the field as of January of 2023. This is not a taxonomy of all displays or systems that use displays, but it is useful for most of the field.
